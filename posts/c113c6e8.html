The Feeling Of University

I’m used to the manner of home in this long summer holiday,so the new environment gave me many terror and I just like to loss myself in the space which I kenw nothing.

To be honest,I very miss my home so that I long to come back home even I kenw It’s a wrong way because now I thought that my small hometown and my room is my all world.

Listening the Lyrical jp-music which I often listen then in my home,the sadness burst into my heart when I wrote this article themed on the feeling of leaving home for new place.

Every coins have tow side.

At the time of remenbering home,I also felt my step was closer to my dream which is my target of my life.

Sadness would be forgiven, and the belief also should be rebuild.Time is observing my step and practice.

I didn’t let my family disappoint to me.Don’t disappoint myself.

The sad student is on a journey.
